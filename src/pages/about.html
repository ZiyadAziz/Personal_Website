ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Ziyad!

I'm a Computer Science graduate from North Carolina State University. I love to program things that let me use my creativity, whether it's making games, designing websites, or building software. As tech constantly evolves, I want to be able to learn as much of it as I can!

I've worked with languages and tools like Java, C++, Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, GameMaker, and Unity to bring my ideas to life. I'm currently seeking opportunities in tech like game development, web development, or anything where I can learn!

When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me exploring new games (currently Elden Ring Nightreign), going hard at the gym, or taking a hike in the woods.
